Our Founding Story

The story of Yash Innovative Solutions LLP began in 1998, when Mr. Sameer Joshi, a visionary civil engineer, founded Yash Associates and Engineers with a singular mission: to offer high-quality, trustworthy project consultancy services that drive real impact on the ground.

What started as a modest consultancy soon gained a reputation for precision, commitment, and client satisfaction. Under Mr. Joshi’s guidance, the company executed several successful projects across infrastructure, water management, and urban development.

Recognizing the growing complexity of projects and the need for innovative, scalable solutions, the firm was restructured in 2017 as Yash Innovative Solutions LLP — carrying forward the original values while expanding into new domains with a future-focused mindset.

Today, we stand as more than just consultants. We are development partners, actively working on transforming villages, rejuvenating lakes, and enabling sustainable, community-driven progress. Our team is involved in end-to-end planning and execution of rural development projects, environmental restoration initiatives, and public infrastructure upgrades.

Driven by the legacy of our founder and powered by a new generation of professionals, Yash Innovative Solutions LLP continues to bridge the gap between vision and execution — with a deep respect for the environment, culture, and people we serve.
